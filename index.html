ATTRIBUTES
  • DEBUT ALBUM YEAR
    Debut album year will turn YELLOW when within 5 years of the mystery artist's debut album year. A down arrow indicates the mystery artists album came out earlier than your guess, while a top arrow indicates it was more recently.
  • GROUP SIZE
    Group Size indicates how many members are in the group (for example, Justin Bieber is group size one, The Beatles are group size four).
  • LISTENER RANK
    The artists ranking on Spotify by streams. An up arrow indicates the mystery artist is more popular than your guess is - down arrow is the oppsite. YELLOW indicates your guess was within 50 of the mystery artist.
  • GENDER
    When the band has men and women, gender will say mixed.
  • GENRE
    We've limited the genres to keep the game simple: Rock, Pop, Hip Hop, Country, and Electronic. This happened cause our friends argued about Green Day being punk or rock.
  • NATIONALITY
    Nationality will turn YELLOW when the nationality of the artist you guessed is on the right continent as the mystery artist's nationality.
